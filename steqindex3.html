Sun Times Equivalence Thing

Thank you for visiting the website of the Sun Times Equivalence Thing! It was one of my past passion projects; I worked on it occasionally from 2018 to 2020, but haven't worked on it since.

The website allows you to input your location and then allows you to choose any day of the year. It will calculate that day's sunrise and sunset times as well of the duration of daylight, and then find other days in the year that have the same sunrise time, sunset time, or duration of daylight. Click here to view the latest version of the Sun Times Equivalence Thing, or look below to see a version history.

You will need an STEQ file for versions prior to July 2019—get yours here!
